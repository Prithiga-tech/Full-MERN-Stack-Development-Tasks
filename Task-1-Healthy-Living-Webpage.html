About Healthy Living

Healthy living means taking care of your body and mind. Eating nutritious food, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep can improve your overall health and happiness.

Healthy Habits

  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Exercise for at least 30 minutes.
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Sleep for 7 to 8 hours daily.
  • Reduce stress through meditation.

Daily Routine

  1. Wake up early.
  2. Go for a morning walk.
  3. Eat a healthy breakfast.
  4. Study or work with focus.
  5. Sleep on time.
